About Me

It's not about being perfect, it's about being real

I was born and raised in Pasadena, California. I studied communications and spanish at UCLA and secondary education at Loyola Marymount. I used to be a teacher, now I am a co-founder for Mackey Creative Lab.

That's my background, but this is who I am. I like to organize things, I like to try to figure stuff out, and I like to work with others. I like when things are neat and tidy, but I keep myself too busy so if you walk into my house mid-week it will probably be messy. I love to talk and laugh, though I usually do both too loudly. I love sports- playing, watching, listening, though I have never nor will never be the next star athlete. I like to sing along to songs, though I usually mess up the words and always sound horrible. I have a big family who are the most important thing to me - even if we are a bunch of weirdos. I love laugh, the ups and the dowms and usually see the best of things.

Work

Get Shit Done

Project Management

Telling people what to do is not being bossy, if it's your job (right?). I spend a lot of time talking to clients and figuring out their project needs. I set dates and deadlines, make lists, and create plans. I do what I can to help see a project from start to finish.

Designing

With no "formal" training, design is something I am trying to develop. Invitations for weddings and parties are my favorite. T-shirts for too many adult kickball teams (yes, you read that right) is what i've done. Website design is what I'm trying to figure out.

Spinning Instructor

Exercise is something I do, not necessarily something I love. But, becoming an indoor cyclying teacher has made it my job-- and I actually like it. I make playlists that are fun and diverse, motivate participants to finish the ride and breake a major sweat while I'm at it. And no, that's not the bike I use.

Creating

I am not a skilled writer - though there's something about grammar rules that I like. When it comes to getting content for a website, I can get it done. I sometimes write it myself. Many times I revise what's already there. The best times are when I just dump in what's already there.
